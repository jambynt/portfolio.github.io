When I moved to the United States back in 2016, I was introduced to a plethora of things I eventually developed an interest in. From photography and filmmaking, to graphic design, and other forms of digital media, my creativity and curiosity explored all the possibilities of what I can create.

Now, I am a junior at Michigan State University majoring in Experience Architecture, and I currently work as a service technician for Apple. In my job, I work directly with users, and I experience first-hand their delights and frustrations with technology. This inspired me to become the person who can change and improve this. In my free time, I love to explore up and coming trends in design and technology.

I believe that the future of design would highly depend on social media trends and the ever-changing aesthetic standards imposed by the internet.

Because design is social.

Design responds to time and society, so we must always keep up with it, unapologetically.
